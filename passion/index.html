what are indie games?

indie or independent games are games made by individuals or small teams without the backing of any major publisher. While they're not often profitable, or even intended to be much of the time, there are many indie games that have become very successful, like:

  • Minecraft
  • Terraria
  • Undertale
  • FNAF
  • and more!
