what are indie games?

indie or independent games are games made by individuals or small teams without the backing of any major publisher. while they're not often profitable, or even intended to be much of the time, here are some successful indie games you may be familiar with:

  • Minecraft
  • Terraria
  • Undertale
  • FNAF
  • and more!

  • alphabetagamer
what is interactive fiction?

"Interactive fiction (IF) is a type of story where the reader or player has some control over how the story progresses. It can be presented as a video game, book, or other media, and can be understood as a literary narrative or a type of game."

this may bring to mind flash player point-and-click adventure games, the Choose Your Own Adventure series of books from the 80s/90s, or bigger AAA games like Until Dawn or Life is Strange.
